Overview

The consolidated engineering practices for how Vibrato does engineering and technical delivery. These standards are agnostic of company, product, tool or technology, but with a view that they can be applied to any company, product, tool or technology. A key assumption in setting context for scoping an engineering effort is that it's bound to a particular individual product.
